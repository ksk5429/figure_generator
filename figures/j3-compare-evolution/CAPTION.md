# j3-compare-evolution

Frequency evolution $\Delta f_1 / f_{1,0}$ against scour depth ratio
$S/D$ for dry vs saturated pairs at matched density. **(a)** Dense
pair — T1 (dry, open circles) vs T4 (saturated, filled circles), No. 7
sand. **(b)** Loose pair — T2 (dry, open squares) vs T5 (saturated,
filled squares). Solid faded lines are linear fits through each
series; the double-headed arrow at the saturated series' maximum
$S/D$ annotates the ratio of interpolated-dry to measured-saturated
decline (the "saturation amplification" of scour sensitivity). Bold
percentages at the deepest scour stage report the absolute decline for
each series.

*Source:* `papers/J3/figure_inputs/effect-saturation-script.parquet`
(shared with `j3-effect-saturation`). This is the
**companion framing** — same data, different visual — that the J3
manuscript uses as fig9_compare_evolution.
