# j3-effect-saturation

Effect of saturation on scour-induced natural-frequency sensitivity for the
J3 centrifuge campaign. **(a)** Matched-pair frequency decline
$|\Delta f_1 / f_{1,0}|$ against scour depth ratio $S/D$ for the dense pair
(T1 dry vs T4 saturated, blue) and the loose pair (T2 dry vs T5 saturated,
coral); shaded bands highlight the dry–saturated gap. Open markers denote
dry tests, filled markers denote saturated tests; amplification arrows
annotate the dry/saturated sensitivity ratio at $S/D \approx 0.40$.
**(b)** Scour sensitivity (absolute slope of frequency decline per unit
scour depth) per test, with the dense and loose dry/saturated ratios
printed above each pair.

*Source:* `papers/J3/figure_inputs/effect-saturation-script.parquet`
(frozen-from-script Tier-2 copy of the hardcoded arrays in
`fig14_effect_saturation.py`, ch5 centrifuge_testing_year2). This is **path
A** of the J3 saturation migration: a faithful reproduction of the
currently-published figure. For a CSV-derived counterpart see
`j3-effect-saturation-verified`.
