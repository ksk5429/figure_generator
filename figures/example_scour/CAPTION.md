# example_scour

**(a)** Synthetic scour profiles as a function of radial distance *r* [m] from
the foundation center: a gravity monopile, the central pile of a tripod
suction bucket foundation, and a tripod leg. Positive *z* [m] indicates
elevation above the seabed (dashed reference line at *z* = 0). Curves were
generated from a Gaussian kernel with additive Gaussian noise (σ = 0.05 m)
and are not calibrated against any physical experiment; the figure exists
only to verify the pipeline end-to-end.

Source: `data/raw/example_scour.csv` (synthetic, seed = 42).
