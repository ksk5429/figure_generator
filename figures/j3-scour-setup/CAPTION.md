# j3-scour-setup

Cross-sectional schematic of the 1:70 centrifuge model installed in
the KAIST beam-centrifuge strongbox (1400 × 700 mm inner, aluminium
with 10 mm Duxseal lining; manuscript Section 2.1). Section plane cuts
through buckets A and B; bucket C is behind B on the line of sight.

**Geometry labels (model / prototype):**
- Strongbox inner width: 1400 mm
- Strongbox inner height: 700 mm
- Tripod base side _L_base = 286 mm / 20.0 m
- Bucket diameter _D_ = 114 mm / 8.0 m
- Skirt length _L_ = 133 mm / 9.3 m
- Example scour depth _S_ = 67 mm (model) corresponding to _S_/_D_
  = 0.58 — the maximum tested in this programme.

**Layers (top-to-bottom):**
- Water column (≈80 mm above soil surface, light blue with wavy
  surface line).
- Saturated sand bed (5 cm = 500 mm at model scale) with
  north-east-hatch pattern.
- Strongbox floor.

**Foundation (drawn in solid near-black):**
- Two visible suction buckets (A left, B right) with vertical skirt
  walls, thick lid bars, and dotted soil-plug interiors.
- Tripod brace frame connecting bucket lids to the central apex.
- Tower stub above the apex with a zigzag break indicating that the
  full 1478 mm model tower + RNA lumped mass continues beyond the
  drawing bounds.

**Scour geometry (dashed accent walls around each bucket):**
- Frustum with 45° side slope.
- Inner floor radius = _D_/2 at the bucket wall.
- Outer rim radius = _D_/2 + _S_ at the original mudline.
- Original mudline shown as faint dashed horizontal reference inside
  each scour hole.

**Shake direction arrow** (upper right) aligns with the A–B axis, as
described in the manuscript for the dynamic excitation protocol.
